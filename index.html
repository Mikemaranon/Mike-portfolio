About me

I like technology when it feels clear, useful and alive.

I am Mike Marañón, a developer with a mixed background in systems, data, web development and applied artificial intelligence. I enjoy understanding how things work from the inside and turning that knowledge into tools that people can actually use.

My path started close to infrastructure, networks and operating systems. Over time, I moved deeper into data engineering and AI, where logic, automation and creativity meet. I care about simple architectures, fast iteration and products that solve real problems without unnecessary noise.

Outside pure code, music and teaching have shaped how I work: I value rhythm, clarity, collaboration and the ability to explain complex ideas without making them heavier than they need to be.
